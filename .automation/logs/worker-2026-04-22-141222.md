## Notes
- `DraftEngine.invalidate(threadID:)` resets all tone variants of a thread to idle DraftState without removing the dict entries; in-flight tasks are cancelled. This differs from `evict()` (which nils out the entries entirely) — keeping the slot means a follow-up `prime()` can restart without allocating new key entries.
- `InboxViewModel` detects new messages via preview string comparison before/after thread replacement. A changed preview is the watcher's signal that a new message arrived. Non-selected threads are not invalidated.
- `ContactsResolver.resolveAll` acquires the lock twice regardless of batch size: once to resolve cache hits and identify misses, once to write miss results back. The store is queried outside the lock.
